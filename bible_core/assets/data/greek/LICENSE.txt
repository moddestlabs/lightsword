TAGNT - Translators Amalgamated Greek New Testament
===================================================

Source: STEPBible.org
License: CC BY 4.0 (Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International)
URL: https://github.com/STEPBible/STEPBible-Data

Description:
All the words from standard Greek editions (NA27/28, TR, SBL, TH, Byz, WH, Treg)
with translations, grammar, sources & variants that affect translation.

Provides enhanced Greek New Testament text with transliteration, English glosses,
disambiguated Strong's numbers, and full morphological analysis for all words.
